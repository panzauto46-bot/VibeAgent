Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Is VibeAgent safe for crypto transactions?

Very safe. We use local AES-256 encryption and are Non-Custodial. Private keys never leave your phone.

How do I start using VibeAgent?

Simply download the APK, then select "Create New Wallet" or "Import Phrase" from your old wallet.

Is there a fee to use VibeAgent services?

Free to use. You only need to pay standard BNB Chain network gas fees for transactions.
